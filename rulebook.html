How It Works

Before the tournament, each player submits predictions for every match score and group standings. Points are awarded based on accuracy as matches are played.

Rules

  • All predictions must be submitted before the first match kicks off (June 11, 2026).
  • No changes after the deadline.
  • The leaderboard updates automatically as results come in.
