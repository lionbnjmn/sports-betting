Rulebook

Point System

Group Stage

  • 1 point for each group match where the exact final score is predicted correctly
    → 72 points total
  • 1 point for each team's correctly predicted position in the group final standings
    → 48 points total

Knockout Stage

Each knockout game has 2ko-round points to win. Half of those points are awarded for correctly picking the winner of the game, and the other half for predicting the match's exact final score.

Point Awards for Knockout Stage
Round Winner Score Points per game Total points per round
Round of 32 1 1 2 32
Round of 16 2 2 4 32
Quarter-final 4 4 8 32
Semi-final 8 8 16 32
Final 16 16 32 32

Notes

  • For matches decided on penalties, we ignore score points since it's annoying to implement and no-one will guess correctly anyway.
  • Also, Momo is barred from winning on accounts of being muslim and knowing nothing about the game.
