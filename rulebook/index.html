Rulebook

Point System

Group Stage

  • 1 point for each group match where the exact final score is predicted correctly
    → 72 points total
  • For each team in a group's final standings, up to 1 point is awarded:
    • 0.5 points for predicting the team's exact finishing position (1st, 2nd, 3rd, or 4th)
    • 0.5 points for correctly placing the team in the top half (1st or 2nd) or bottom half (3rd or 4th) of the group
    → 48 points total

Knockout Stage

Each knockout game has 2ko-round points to win. Half of those points are awarded for correctly picking the winner of the game, and the other half for predicting the match's exact final score.

Point Awards for Knockout Stage
Round Winner Score Points per game Total points per round

Notes

  • For knockout matches, the score half-point is awarded based on the score after extra time (or after 90 minutes if no extra time was played). The winner half-point reflects who actually advanced, including via penalties.
  • Momo is barred from winning on accounts of being Muslim and knowing nothing about the game.
